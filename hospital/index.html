Aboutus

Welcome to Green Life Hospital Ltd. On 27 June 2005, Dr. Md. Mainul Ahasan, Managing Director coordinated and organized about 50 distinguished physicians and Surgeons of the Country and started a Hospital to provide specialized health care services to all types of patients especially poor patients in the private sector named Green Life Hospital Ltd. It is an International Standard well equipped unique Multi-Disciplinary hospital in Bangladesh that has been working with the maximum number of eminent own physicians and surgeons in the country. We are confidently providing comprehensive health care service to the patients with the latest Medical, Surgical, Diagnostic, and Blood Transfusion facilities by expert medical professionals, skilled nurses, and technologists using state-of-the-art technology.
